About the Team

Hapoel Holon is a professional basketball club based in Holon, Israel. The team competes in the Israeli Premier League and has established itself as one of the most competitive teams in Israeli basketball.

Home Arena: Holon Toto Hall
Capacity: 5,500
Team Colors: Purple and White
Founded: 1947

History

The team has a rich history in Israeli basketball, representing the city of Holon with pride and distinction since its establishment in 1947.

Notable Achievements

  • Israeli Championship titles
  • State Cup victories
  • Regular participation in European competitions
